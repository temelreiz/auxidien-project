# 7. TOKENOMICS

Auxidien (AUXI) is a fixed-supply, volume-weighted global precious metals index token. The supply is permanently capped, and no additional minting is possible after deployment.

## 7.1 Total Supply

The total supply is permanently established as follows:

```
Total Supply:        100,000,000 AUXI
Minting:             Only at deployment
Additional minting:  Disabled
Burn:                None (V1)
```

Auxidien is not a stablecoin, asset-backed token, or reserve-based instrument. It is a pure index-based investment token.

## 7.3 Team Token Lock (On-chain Vesting)

The Team & Development allocation is locked by a smart contract and cannot be accessed outside the vesting schedule.

### Vesting Schedule

```
Vesting Start:           Deployment + 30 days
Cliff:                   6 months after start
Total vesting duration:  36 months (3 years)
Release model:           Linear unlock after cliff
```

The allocation is split across three separate beneficiary addresses.

Tokens can be released only by:
- The respective beneficiary
- The contract owner

### Release Timeline

| Period | Cumulative Unlock |
|--------|-------------------|
| Month 0-6 | 0% (Cliff) |
| Month 7 | ~3.3% |
| Month 12 | ~20% |
| Month 24 | ~60% |
| Month 36 | 100% |

## 7.4 Strategic & Advisors Vesting

The Strategic & Advisors allocation is also subject to vesting via separate smart contracts.

### Initial Policy

```
Cliff:    3-6 months
Vesting:  12-24 months
```

This structure ensures long-term alignment and risk protection.

## 7.5 Treasury Policy

Treasury funds are:
- Stored in a multisig wallet
- Used for listing fees, oracle services, legal and operational expenses
- Subject to transparency policies
- Reported quarterly to the community

## 7.6 Liquidity & Market Making

Liquidity allocation is used for:
- Centralized exchange market making
- Decentralized exchange liquidity pools
- Price stabilization mechanisms
- Cross-chain bridge liquidity
